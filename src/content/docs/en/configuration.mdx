We have put in a lot of effort to ensure that the Yabe Heikou plugin is user-friendly. As such, the configuration is simple and straightforward, and then forget about it.

The Yabe Heikou plugin is powered by Pusher Channel service on its core, allowing it to achieve real-time experience in economic and easy to setup.

To get started, you need a Pusher Channel App.

## Creating a Pusher Channel App

### Step 1: Login to Pusher Channel Dashboard

Login to your Pusher Channel dashboard at [https://dashboard.pusher.com](https://dashboard.pusher.com).

<a data-fslightbox href="/tutorial/configuration/1.png">
![](/tutorial/configuration/1.png)
</a>

### Step 2: Access the Channels product dashboard

Click on the Channels **Get Started** button.

<a data-fslightbox href="/tutorial/configuration/2.png">
![](/tutorial/configuration/2.png)
</a>

### Step 3: Create a new Channels app

Click on the **Create app** button to create a new Channels app.

<a data-fslightbox href="/tutorial/configuration/3.png">
![](/tutorial/configuration/3.png)
</a>


### Step 4: Create a new Channels app

<a data-fslightbox href="/tutorial/configuration/4.png">
![](/tutorial/configuration/4.png)
</a>

1. On the cluster field, choose the region that is closest to you.
2. Click on the **Create app** button to create the new Channels app.

### Step 5: Client events

To make the data sync work between the Oxygen editors, you need to enable the client events on the Channels app.

Navigate to the **App Settings** tab.
On the **Client Events** section, click the **Enable client events** button.

<a data-fslightbox href="/tutorial/configuration/5.png">
![](/tutorial/configuration/5.png)
</a>

## Configuring Yabe Heikou

### Step 1: Open the Yabe Heikou plugin settings

On your WordPress website, go to **Oxygen → Yabe Heikou** and and switch to the **PUSHER CHANNELS** tab.

<a data-fslightbox href="/tutorial/configuration/7.png">
![](/tutorial/configuration/7.png)
</a>

### Step 2: Paste the App Keys

Paste the App Keys you copied from the previous step ([Creating a Pusher Channel App — Step 6: App Keys](#step-6-app-keys)) by clicking the <code><i class="fa-solid fa-paste"></i> Paste App Keys</code> button. It will automatically fill the form.

<a data-fslightbox href="/tutorial/configuration/8.png">
![](/tutorial/configuration/8.png)
</a>

### Step 3: Save the settings

Click the **Save Changes** button to save the settings.

<a data-fslightbox href="/tutorial/configuration/9.png">
![](/tutorial/configuration/9.png)
</a>

## Next Step

After configuring the Yabe Heikou plugin, the feature will now run on the Oxygen editor.
